Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) Hazards:

Hazard: Drones flying over construction sites may collide with workers or equipment.

Solution: Establish no-fly zones, require operator certification, and implement drone tracking and warning systems.

Construction Noise Pollution:

Hazard: Excessive noise levels affecting both workers and nearby residents.

Solution: Use noise barriers, schedule noisy work during permitted hours, and provide noise-cancelling headsets for workers.

Structural Collapse Hazards:

Hazard:Structural failure during construction.

Solution: Ensure proper design and engineering of structures. Conduct regular structural inspections.

Electrical Safety Hazards:

Hazard: Risk of electrical accidents, such as electric shocks and fires, on construction sites.

Solution: Implement strict electrical safety protocols. Ensure proper grounding and insulation of electrical equipment. Provide training on electrical safety for all workers.
